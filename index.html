Welcome to Lakeshore

Nurturing Minds, Building Character & Shaping Bright Futures

At LAKESHORE English Medium School, we believe that high-quality education should be accessible to every family. Located in the heart of Old Mancherial, Telangana, we provide disciplined academics, caring faculty, and concept-based learning for Nursery to 7th Standard students.

Our dedicated teaching staff focuses on individual attention, communication skills, confidence building, and holistic growth through academics, sports, and cultural activities.

Academic Programs

Nurturing Excellence From Nursery to 7th Class

We provide a structured English Medium curriculum that makes learning an enjoyable journey of discovery, prioritizing conceptual clarity over rote learning.

Nursery, LKG, and UKG

The early years are critical for a child's brain development. Our kindergarten program focuses on play-way and activity-oriented methods to build early vocabulary, cognitive agility, and physical coordination.

Activity-Based Learning

Learning through games, shapes, drawing, and colors.

Social & Emotional Development

Focusing on sharing, group interaction, and discipline.

Phonics & Basic Arithmetic

Strong foundations in language pronunciation and numbering.

Hygienic Playrooms

A safe, sanitized environment with age-appropriate toys.

Primary Classes (1st to 5th Standard)

Our primary classes emphasize concept-based learning. We aim to spark curiosity and develop a analytical mindset in core subjects: English, Mathematics, General Sciences, Social Studies, and Regional Languages.

Concept-Focused Syllabus

Ensuring students understand the 'why' behind mathematical and scientific concepts.

Language & Communication

Intensive focus on reading fluency, handwriting, and speaking in English.

Regular Assessments

Frequent chapter-wise reviews to identify and correct learning gaps early.

Value-Based Lessons

Moral science and ethics integrated into daily learning to foster respect and empathy.

Middle School Classes (6th to 7th Standard)

Preparing children for higher-level challenges. We introduce structured scientific investigations, advanced mathematical problem-solving, and analytical writing to build ultimate academic confidence.

Advanced Problem Solving

Strengthening mathematics foundation in algebra, geometry, and arithmetic.

Confidence & Public Speaking

Encouraging classroom presentations, debates, and leadership roles.

Disciplined Academic Focus

Regular homework, study schedules, and exams designed for preparation.

Mentorship Programs

Individual teachers assigned to guide and monitor the career goals of older children.
